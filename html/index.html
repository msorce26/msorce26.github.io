SNAKE 3 REMASTERED

The snake is back and hungrier than ever! Get ready to take on the classic arcade game like never before! Begin your journey and maneuver through the harsh and unforgiving terrain in search for sustenance. Continue to eat so that you may grow strong enough to defeat any foe that may cross your path. Yet, make sure not to wonder too far from home, or you will surely parish to the unknown.

This fast-passed game will test your awareness, reflexes, and your drive to be the best gosh darn snake there ever was. But beware, as many who play this game soon realize, your biggest obstacle is often yourself...
